SLR 670

Camera Details

Improved SX-70 camera for added functionality thanks to the Time Machine module and improved electronics.

Auto exposure is compatible with Polaroid SX-70 and 600 type films. Manual shutter speeds range from 1/2000s to 1s. Additional Bulb and Time shutter options are available.

Fixed 116mm focal length, fixed f/8 aperture lens with glass elements. Manual focusing with Split Circle focusing screen.

The camera compacts to a thin form factor for enhanced portability. The Time Machine module can be removed from the camera for additional compactness or to use a MiNT flash bar (sold separately).

The SLR670-s Classic DOESN'T HAVE a tripod mount and neck strap hooks. The camera is powered via the film cartridge.
